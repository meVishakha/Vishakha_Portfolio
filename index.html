About Me

Passionate designer with a love for typography and visual storytelling

Hello! I'm Vishakha, a self-taught graphic designer who believes in the power of thoughtful typography and intentional layouts. My journey began with curiosity and has evolved through countless hours of experimentation and play.

I specialize in creating visual narratives that connect with audiences through clean aesthetics and strategic design thinking. From branding to social media, each project is an opportunity to tell a unique story.
